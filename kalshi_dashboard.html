THE SPORTS PARADOX
90%
Sports drives ~85% of Kalshi volume yet shows 90% avg spreads — the highest-volume category is simultaneously the most illiquid. Median spread of 86% confirms this is structural, not statistical noise.
SCIENCE/TECH BIMODAL DISTRIBUTION
15%
Median spread of 15% vs mean of 51% reveals a bimodal distribution — a liquid core of well-followed tech events surrounded by a long tail of illiquid niche markets dragging the average up.
ENTERTAINMENT STRUCTURAL BREAKDOWN
127%
Median of 127% exceeds mean of 98% — the distribution skews toward extreme illiquidity. Unlike Sports, there is no liquid core. Most entertainment markets are worse than the average suggests.
MACRO/POLITICS EFFICIENCY
~20%
Both categories show median spreads near 20% — genuine efficiency driven by informed participants with real information edges on Fed decisions, election outcomes, and economic data releases.
